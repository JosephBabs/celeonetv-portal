# CeleOne - Public App Guide

## Welcome to CeleOne
CeleOne is a mobile and web platform for the Celestial Christian community.  
It helps users receive trusted information, follow major decisions, join community discussions, watch live channels, and access useful spiritual content.

## Why CeleOne exists
CeleOne was created to:
- limit the spread of false information
- centralize official announcements and decisions
- improve transparency and community understanding
- provide a respectful and useful exchange space

## What you can do on CeleOne

### 1. Follow official information
- read news and reforms
- understand important community decisions
- share content with clear previews on WhatsApp and social platforms

### 2. Participate in community life
- join discussions through chatrooms
- comment constructively
- contribute to public debate respectfully

### 3. Watch live channels
- access available TV/Web TV channels
- watch live streams from each channel page
- follow religious and community broadcasts

### 4. Access verified hymns and documents
- search hymns
- consult verified church documents
- read essential content organized by category

### 5. Participate in community competitions
Example: "Amis de Jesus" (children bible competition)
- candidate registrations
- official result publication
- result search inside the app

## Supported languages
CeleOne is designed for a multilingual audience:
- French
- English
- Yoruba
- Spanish

A language selector is available at the top of the site for easier reading.

## Opportunities for artists and filmmakers
CeleOne provides a professional space for creators:
- musical artists
- video creators and filmmakers
- TV/Web TV broadcasters

Benefits:
- content distribution to a targeted audience
- stronger community visibility
- monetization opportunities based on usage

## Information protection and security
CeleOne applies security principles to protect users and data:
- role-based access management
- moderation of sensitive content
- priority to trusted sources
- tracking of important updates

## Good-use policy (chatrooms and content)
To keep a healthy platform:
- respect people and beliefs
- no hate, harassment, or defamation
- no fake official announcements
- moderation and sanctions in case of abuse

## CeleOne commitment
CeleOne is committed to:
- delivering reliable and useful information
- supporting the community and youth
- empowering creators (music, film, media)
- building a responsible and sustainable digital space

## Need help?
If you need support:
- use official platform support channels
- report inappropriate content
- contact the team for questions about results, documents, or live channels
